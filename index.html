NYC DOB Compliance

Stay ahead of every DOB deadline

NYC elevator fines add up fast. Concord keeps complete records on every device under our care, files with licensed inspectors on time, and stays current on the city's ever-changing rules — so you're never left in the dark.

  • Category 1 annual no-load testing and Category 5 five-year load tests, filed with the DOB within the 14-day window
  • Door-lock monitoring and 2027 emergency-brake readiness reviewed on every modernization
  • Violation removal, expediting, and ECB hearing representation from start to finish
