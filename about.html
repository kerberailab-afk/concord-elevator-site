About Us

A smaller company that treats you bigger

Concord is a NYC fully licensed and insured company, servicing the five boroughs and beyond for over 30 years. We take pride in being a smaller, more personable business — competitive rates, high-quality service, and an assessment and estimate within 24 hours.

Our promise

You'll never be treated as just a number

From our customer service to our field mechanics, you will never be treated as just a number. A tenant suddenly trapped on a passenger car; a delivery delayed by a downed freight elevator; a car lift backing up your garage customers — we do care. We are an emergency service, and we act as such.

Not only do we have the expertise to handle mechanical issues, we also have an expediting team offering prompt violation removal, including handling ECB hearings. From the bottom to the top, we have the knowledge to see to any and all of your elevator needs.
