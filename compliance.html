We know just how costly NYC Department of Buildings fines can be. Concord keeps complete records on every device under our care, files every inspection on time, and stays current on the city's ever-changing rules.

Category 1 & Category 5 Inspections

Inspections filed on time, every time

NYC requires every elevator to be inspected and tested twice in the cycle — an annual Category 1 no-load safety test and a Category 5 load-and-speed test every five years. Reports must reach the DOB within 14 days. We perform inspections with licensed inspectors and handle the filing for you.

  • Category 1 annual no-load safety testing
  • Category 5 five-year load & speed testing
  • Third-party witnessing and on-time DOB filing
Violation Removal & ECB Hearings

From the bottom to the top, we clear it

If a violation is issued, our expediting team provides a prompt remedy — sending supporting documentation to the DOB on your behalf to avert further fines. We also represent you at ECB hearings, so you don't face the process alone.

Know your deadlines

The NYC dates that cost you if you miss them

A quick map of the requirements we track for every building under our care. We'll tell you exactly where your equipment stands.

Annual

Category 1 inspection & test

A no-load safety test required once per calendar year, with the report filed to the DOB within 14 days of inspection.

5 Years

Category 5 inspection & test

A rated load and speed safety test required within five years of the last Cat 5 (or of a new elevator's final certificate).

In effect

Door-lock monitoring

Existing elevators must monitor car-door position to prevent movement with an open door. We verify and address this on every modernization.

Jan 2027

Emergency brake (second means)

An upcoming requirement for a second emergency brake on existing elevators. We assess readiness now so there are no last-minute surprises.

Dates reflect NYC Department of Buildings requirements as we track them and can change. We confirm the exact status and deadlines for your specific devices.
