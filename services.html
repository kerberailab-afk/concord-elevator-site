Maintenance

Preventive maintenance that prevents downtime

NYC requires a licensed contractor to perform regular maintenance and keep logs on site for DOB review. We do exactly that — scheduled service that catches small issues before they strand a passenger or shut down a freight car.

  • Routine monthly maintenance with on-site logs kept current
  • Full tracking of every device under our care
  • Passenger, freight, dumbwaiter and hydraulic equipment
Repair & Emergency Service

A real emergency service — and we act like it

A tenant trapped in a passenger car, a delivery delayed by a downed freight elevator, a car lift backing up your garage — we care, and we respond around the clock. Fast diagnosis, honest answers, and the right fix.

  • 24/7 emergency response for entrapments and shutdowns
  • Prompt repairs by highly qualified, licensed mechanics
  • Fair, transparent pricing with no surprise line items
Modernization

Modernize for safety, savings and ride quality

Aging controllers, doors and fixtures cost you in reliability, energy and code compliance. We modernize equipment to current NYC standards — including door-lock monitoring and the 2027 emergency-brake requirement — so your building is safer and future-ready.

  • Controller, door operator and fixture upgrades
  • Brought up to current NYC Building Code
  • Smoother, quieter, more energy-efficient rides
